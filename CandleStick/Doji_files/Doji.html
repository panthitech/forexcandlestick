DOJI


Doji pattern

If the open and close of the price are equal or very close to each other with a small wick then those patterns are called Doji. These are the strongest and Reversal patterns.

Doji patterns
  • Gravestone- it is a Bearish candle, when at the top of a short term rally
  • Dragonfly- is Bullish when at the bottom of a short term break
  • Long-Legged- shows Indecision
  • Four Price- usually seen in illiquid or low volume markets, no trades may have took place

Gravestone

Fig1: Gravestone Doji
  • Gravestones must be seen at the top of a uptrend market
  • There could be a small wick or very small body
  • For a short time, this candle can act as a reversal and strong bearish pattern
  • Trade short below the lows of this candle or next candle
  • This must be followed by a confirmation of next candle to confirm trade

Dragonfly

Dragonfly Doji
  • It is opposite to the Gravestone candle
  • Dragonfly candles are seen at the bottom of a Downtrend market
  • There could be a small wick or very small body
  • For a short time, this candle can act as a reversal and strong Bullish trend
  • Trade long above the highs of this candle or next candle
  • This must be followed by a confirmation of next candle to confirm trade

Long-Legged

Long-Legged Doji
  • As you can notice the candle, traders are indecision
  • These are common in sideways market
  • Do not take any trade, just wait for other opportunity

Four Price

Four Price
  • Open, High, Low and Close, all prices are same
  • Usually there wont be any trade in this candle
  • These candles usually forms in very low volume markets
  • Do not take any trade
