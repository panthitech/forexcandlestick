Short Candles


Shorts

These candles have small body with short shadows. These are very less potential. They usually found in sideways market.

Short Candle
  • Shows small range between the opening and closing price
  • Small Shadows
  • Multiple short days can indicate consolidation
  • These just move along with the current trend
  • Do not take any decision based on this candle
