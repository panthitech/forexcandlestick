ENGULFING


Engulfing Candles

The Engulfing candlestick is a multiple candle pattern that signal a reversal of the ongoing trend in the market.

Fig: Engulfing Candles

This candlestick pattern involves two candles, in which latter candle ‘engulfing’ the entire body of the candle before it. Engulfing candle can be bullish or bearish depending on the existing trend.

In simple terms, engulfing means cover or eat completely. Here second candle covers fist candle completely. So the name Engulfing Candles.


There are two types in engulfing candle patterns based on market trend

1. Bullish engulfing pattern

The bullish engulfing candle indicates reversal of a downtrend and tells us that increase in buying pressure when it appears at the bottom of a downtrend.

The pattern involves two candles with the second green candle that is completely engulfing the body of the previous red candle.

Fig: Bullish Engulfing Candles
  • The bullish engulfing candle found at the bottom of the downtrend it signals uptrend reversal
  • First candle is Red and second candle is Green and larger in size
  • Indicates bullish reversal
  • It can be used as a signal to exit if the trader holds a buying or selling position in the ongoing trend that is coming to an end.
  • The bullish engulfing fails when a new Low is achieved after the second candle.
  • It doesn't provide any target, but it tells that traders are interested to go opposite to the down trend, means trend reversal might occur
  • The bullish engulfing candle's Low or close can be considered as Support line


2. Bearish engulfing pattern

The bearish engulfing pattern is the opposite of the bullish pattern.

The bearish engulfing candle indicates reversal of a uptrend and this pattern triggers a reversal of the ongoing trend as more sellers enter the market and they make the prices fall.

The pattern involves two candles with the second Red candle that is completely engulfing the body of the previous Green candle.

Fig: Bearish engulfing Candles
  • The Bearish engulfing candle found at the top of the uptrend it signals downtrend reversal
  • First candle is Green and second candle is Red in color, and larger in size
  • Indicates Bearish reversal
  • It can be used as a signal to exit if the trader holds a buying or selling position in the ongoing trend that is coming to an end.
  • The bearish engulfing fails when a new High is achieved after the second candle.
  • It doesn't provide any target, but it tells that traders are interested to go opposite to the uptrend, means trend reversal might occur
  • The bearish engulfing candle's High or close can be considered as Support line
