Spinning Top

This candlestick is a continuation pattern and some times acts as reversal pattern. This occurs when buyers and sellers balance each other out, resulting in similar opening and closing price levels.

If the spinning top is seen at the bottom of a downtrend, it could mean that a bullish reversal might happen. Similarly, if it occurs at the top of an uptrend, it could signal bearish reversal.

Spinning Top
  • The shadows are longer than the body
  • Shows indecision
  • Also seen in consolidation
  • Helpful in determining whether a price reversal might occur
  • Do not take any blind decision based on this pattern. Better to wait for next candle or use technical analysis thereafter.
