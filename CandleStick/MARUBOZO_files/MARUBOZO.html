MARUBOZO

It is a complete body candlestick. Open and Close price will be same as the Low and High. A very small shadow/wick can form.

MARUBOZO
  • Shows large range between the opening and closing price with immediate strength or weakness
  • Usually the second bar in a reversal or continuation pattern
  • When the candle after the Marubozu opens in green, it's confirmation of positive uptrend. You can take a buy position.
  • The Top/bottom price of the Marubozu candle is the stop loss, determined based on trend. If the price goes above/below this after you take the trade - sell and exit.
  • Volume on the day the Marubozu has been formed is significantly higher than the other days. This is a positive sign.
  • Has no or very small shadows or wick
  • Has only body
  • This candle has lesser potential than compared to other candlestick patterns mentioned in this tutorial app. So Do not trade blindly.

Note: Please do not enter into trade only based on Marubozu candle. Use any other indicators for the confirmation.
