Long Candlestick


Long candles

These are larger in shape and may has short shadows. These represents the potential of the market.

Long Candles
  • Shows large range between the opening and closing price
  • Shows strength and weakness of the market
  • Forms in trend markets
  • Bulk buy or sell forms this candle
  • Gives confirmation of the trend
  • Most of the time, Market try retest the previous High or Low
