Rounded Pattern

The Rounded Pattern is a reversal chart pattern, which develops after a price decline. As a stock is trending lower, the rate of the decline will begin to slow down. This is followed by a range pattern, which ultimately shifts into a slow gradual increase. This increase ultimately leads to a bullish move.

A Rounded Pattern looks similar to the cup and handle pattern, but does not experience the temporary downward/upward trend of the "handle" portion. Rounded Pattern are reversal, whereas Cup and handle pattern are continuation pattern.

Once the Rounded pattern is complete, the stock breaks out and will continue in its new upward/downward trend.

There are two types in Rounded pattern


1. Rounded Bottom:
Rounded Bottom

In these patterns, price gradually switches from bearish to bullish


2. Rounded Top:
Rounded Top

In these patterns, price gradually switches from bullish to bearish


Timeframe:
  • Applys to or effective on Long term timeframe
How to Trade:
  • Identify the Rounded Bottom/Top Figure
  • To confirm the pattern, you need to find a price decrease/increase, which slowly switches to a range followed by a price increase/decrease respectivly.
  • The strongest confirmation comes when the volume indicator shows high volumes on the decline, flat volumes on the range and increasing volumes on the reversal.
  • Volume is a key indicator for identifying and validating the rounding bottom pattern.
  • Draw a horizontal line(Neck Line) across the top of the bearish and bullish sides of the rounding bottom pattern
  • As the close price crosses the neck line in a reversal direction with large candle and increased volume
  • This confirms the breakout
Target TAKE PROFIT:
  • Measure the distance between the neck line and the lowest/highest point of the pattern and then apply the same distance to the downside/upside starting from the Neck Line respectively. This would be your minimum target profit.

Stop LOSS:
  • Stop level should be slightly above/below of the neck line in the pattern respectively
