Head and Shoulders

Head and Shoulders patterns are one of the most important of reversal patterns because they are both common and reliable. The head and shoulder top pattern consists of 3 price peaks and a breakout.

  • The Head
  • Left shoulder (LS)
  • Right shoulder (RS)

The center peak(head) is highest and other two peaks lower giving it the shape of a head and shoulders.

The left and right shoulder peaks are almost of same price level or trend level zone and same distance from the head.

Horizontal line connecting the lows of the left shoulder, the head and the right shoulder, you get a support line.

There are two types of Head and Shoulders


1. Head and Shoulders:
Head and Shoulders

These pattern are formed in uptrends and these are bearish reversal trading pattern


2. Inverse Head & Shoulders:
Inverse Head and Shoulders

Inverse Head and Shoulders are the mirror/opposite to the normal Head and Shoulders pattern

These pattern are formed in bear trends and these are bullish reversal trading pattern


Timeframe:
  • Applys to all timeframe
How to Trade:
  • Identify the trend
  • Spot the head and shoulder, draw the Neck Line connecting the lows\highs respectively
  • Wait for the Neckline Breakout
  • If the close price cross the Neck Line with a big candlesticks, This confirm the breakout
  • Increase in volume near the Neck line determines the perfect breakout
Target TAKE PROFIT:
  • Measure the size of the pattern(height) from the neckline to the Head high/low and then apply the same distance to the downside/upside starting from the Neck Line respectively. This would be your minimum target profit.
Entry & Exit
Stop LOSS:
  • Stop level should be slightly above/below of the right shoulder in the pattern respectively
